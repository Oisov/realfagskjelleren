Purpose and Goals

The purpose of the website is to give students a better insight into Realfagskjelleren. If you are wondering about the opening hours, which upcoming events there are, or applying for a working position to work there.

Audience

The targeted audience is students who belong to one of the four student unions running Realfagskjelleren. Thus, the website is targeted at students from Volvox \& Alkymisten (Chemists and Biologists), Online (Computer Scientists), Spanskrøret (Teachers) and Delta (Mathematicians and Physisists).
