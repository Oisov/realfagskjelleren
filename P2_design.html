Our Client

  • Client Name: Realfagskjelleren
  • Contact Person: Andreas F. Eikeland, the leader of Realfagskjelleren

Purpose and Goals

The purpose of the website is to serve as a informationhub for Realfagskjelleren. The website should be able to answer the majority of questions curious visitors may have regarding opening hours, upcoming events and posibilities for work. To reach this goal the website requires a way to display past and future events and an about site with contact information. Another goal is to increase interest in Realfagskjelleren's events through the website. In that regard, the website requires a way to show media.

Audience

The targeted audience is students, mainly those who belong to one of the four student unions running Realfagskjelleren. Most students have a need for human interaction, and Realfagskjelleren serves as a social gathering place where everyone is welcome.

Content of the Website and Organization

  1. Home

    2. The home page of the website. Here you find a brief introduction of Realfagskjelleren with a big logo. On the right side of the page we will have a few of the coming events listed. At the top of the page, a menu with the different sections of the website will be shown. This menu will be shown on all the other sections as well.

  2. Schedule

    3. A month-based calendar showing events will fill the center of the page. Here students can see what is going on this weekend and the coming weekends.

  3. About

    4. A brief overview of Realfagskjelleren's history, its members and rules.

  4. Media

    5. A selection of pictures and videos from events in the basement! It will be a grid of pictures covering the whole page. When you click on a picture or video it will expand and cover the screen.

  5. Contact us

    6. A short page with contact information of a select few individuals

Final Location of the other Website

Our initial plan is to use Github to host the site, as each person is allowed to host one simple website. There is currently no further plan for a future location

Technical Difficulties

We would like to have a calendar of which events Realfagskjelleren will be hosting. These are added on google calendar, however this is also used to schedule meetings and etc. Thus, one challenge will be to extract the correct events.
